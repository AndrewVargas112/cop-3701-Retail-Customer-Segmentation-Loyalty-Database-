# cop-3701-Retail-Customer-Segmentation-Loyalty-Database-
Retail Customer Segmentation &amp; Loyalty Database 


The purpose of this database is to determine the loyalty of customers by collecting data on how 
they shop, in the sense of how long they are there, what they buy, and how often they come back. 
This will also be able to tell how likely they are to remain a regular customer. Companies that 
would likely use would be a supermarket/mart, local stores, and resturants.
